    1. Numei paktatte' kampau khuaizu bangin khum mahmahin a thugente uh a neel dakdak hangin, 4 a tawpna-ah lungkhamna leh natna simloh nangma tungah bangmah dang hong guanlo hi. 5 Amah in sihna gamah nang hong paipih ding a, ama tot lampi pen sihna mun tun'na lampi ahi hi. 6 Nuntakna lampi-ah amah omlo a, nang na phawkloh hangin amah lam pialin a vakvai ahi hi.
    2. Na sih kuan uh ciangin thumin na tau ding uh a, gawngin na guhguak bekun na om ding uh hi.
    3. Bang hang hiam cih leh anlum khat tawh numei paktat khat kingahzo a, ahi zongin numei tawh mawhna in nuntakna bup siasak hi. 27 Na ang sungah meikuang pom lecin na puan kangloin om ding ahi hiam? 28 Mei-am tung tawnin pai lecin na khe kangloin om ding ahi hiam?
    4. Ahi zongin numei tawh a mawhte pen thu ngaihsun theilo mi hi a, amau leh amau a kisusiatawm ahi uh hi.
    5. Tua bang numei in mi tampi tak siasakin, simzawhloh ding mi tampi' sihna a piangsak hi. 27 Tua bang numei zuihna pen sihna gam zuatna hi a, sih baihna ding lampi zuatna ahi hi.
    6. Numei tawh mawhna na pelh un. Mi khat in a bawl mawhna tuamtuamte in ama pumpi sukhalo hi, a ci om hi. Ahi zongin numei tawh a mawhte, ama pumpi tungah a khial ahi hi.
